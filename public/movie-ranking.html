The Best Movies According To Arattu Annan

My Top 3 movies of all-time

Premalu

Premalu (transl. Love) is a 2024 Indian Malayalam-language romantic comedy film[7] directed by Girish A. D. and produced by Bhavana Studios, in the company of Fahadh Faasil and Friends and Working Class Hero.

Aavesham

Aavesham (transl. Excitement) is a 2024 Indian Malayalam-language action comedy film directed by Jithu Madhavan and produced by Nazriya Nazim and Anwar Rasheed under the banner of Fahadh Faasil and Friends and Anwar Rasheed Entertainments.

Varshangalkku Shesham

Varshangalkku Shesham (transl. Years later) is a 2024 Indian Malayalam-language period comedy drama film based on the South Indian Film Industry, written and directed by Vineeth Sreenivasan.
